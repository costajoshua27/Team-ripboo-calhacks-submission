Our Mission

reFresh was founded by a group of passionate college students looking to revolutionize the way we cook and use our ingredients. Today, America constantly faces a problem of food waste, and this waste of food contributes to the acceleration of global warming as more produce is needed to maintain the same levels of demand. According to the Atlantic, "the average value of discarded produce is nearly $1,600 annually" for an American family of four. At reFresh, we believe that this level of waste is unacceptable in our modern society, and we propose a solution to solve this issue.

Our Product

At reFresh, we've worked hard to provide you with the best possible way to reduce your waste by giving you ways to use your leftover ingredients that you buy to cook your meals. reFresh offers the ability to add your ingredients and find recipes that use those ingredients and other recipes that use your leftover ingredients from previous recipes. We also offer a way to find recipes that you want. In addition, we provide an easy way to see how much of an ingredient you need and the cost of those ingredients.
